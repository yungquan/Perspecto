Does a 3D angle work for app store guidelines

Both Apple and Google allow stylized presentation as long as the actual app UI is represented accurately and remains legible. A gentle 3D tilt - rather than an extreme angle - keeps your screenshot compliant while adding visual interest compared to a flat rectangle.

Recommended workflow for app screenshots

  • Take your raw app screenshot at the device's native resolution
  • Upload it to Perspecto and choose the Floating or Hero preset for a subtle, readable tilt
  • Keep rotation modest - extreme angles can make small UI text harder to read in store thumbnails
  • Export at the highest resolution available for crisp display on high-DPI phone screens

Batch processing multiple screens

If you're preparing a full set of App Store screenshots, Perspecto's batch export feature (Pro) lets you apply the same 3D angle to several screens at once and download them all as a ZIP, saving significant time compared to adjusting each screenshot individually in a design tool.
