Why a 3D mockup helps your launch

Product Hunt galleries are crowded with flat screenshots. A 3D angled mockup stands out in the thumbnail grid and signals more polish before anyone reads your tagline. The isometric left-tilt angle has become the de facto standard in the maker community because it shows enough of the UI to be readable while still looking dimensional.

How to make your Product Hunt mockup

  • Upload your app's main screen as a PNG or JPG screenshot
  • Click the Product Hunt quick preset to apply the standard isometric angle instantly
  • Adjust Layer Depth for a more pronounced floating effect if you want extra polish
  • Export as PNG with a transparent or dark background depending on your gallery style

Tips for launch day visuals

Keep your first gallery image as the 3D hero shot - it's what most visitors see first in the feed. Use flat screenshots for the remaining gallery images so reviewers can still read details clearly. Export at 2x or higher if you have Pro, since Product Hunt displays images at high pixel density on modern screens.
