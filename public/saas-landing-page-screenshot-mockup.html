Why angled product shots convert better

A subtle 3D tilt adds depth and draws the eye toward your interface without distracting from your headline and CTA. Most landing page builders and design agencies use this technique because it reads as more premium than a flat rectangle screenshot, while still keeping the UI fully legible.

Best settings for a hero section

The Hero preset is tuned specifically for this use case - a gentle tilt that keeps your interface readable at a glance. Enable the Browser Chrome Frame toggle if your app is a web product, so visitors immediately recognize it as a website rather than a native app. Export with a transparent background so it blends seamlessly into any landing page color scheme.

Exporting for web performance

For landing pages, export at 2x resolution for retina screens, then compress the PNG with a tool like TinyPNG before uploading - this keeps your hero image sharp without slowing down page load, which also affects your SEO and conversion rate.
