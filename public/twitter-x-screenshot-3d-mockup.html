Why angle matters in a fast-scrolling feed

X's timeline moves fast, and a flat rectangular screenshot blends into the stream of other flat screenshots. A subtle 3D tilt creates just enough visual difference to catch the eye mid-scroll without looking gimmicky, which matters most for threads meant to drive replies and reposts.

Good presets for X posts

The Product Hunt isometric preset and the Floating preset both read well at the smaller in-feed image size X displays. Avoid extreme rotation since small UI text can become hard to read once an image is scaled down in the timeline.

Quote-tweet and thread strategy

A consistent 3D angle across a launch thread - same rotation, same depth - gives the whole thread a cohesive, designed feel rather than a random collection of screenshots, which is a small detail that compounds over a multi-tweet thread.
