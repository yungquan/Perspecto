Why visuals outperform text-only posts on LinkedIn

Posts with an image consistently get more impressions and engagement than text-only updates, and a custom angled mockup reads as more intentional than a plain screenshot pasted in. It signals you put effort into the announcement, which matters for things like funding posts, feature launches, or milestone updates.

Best mockup style for LinkedIn

The Hero or Floating presets work well here since LinkedIn's image preview crops differently than Twitter or Product Hunt - a centered, slightly tilted mockup with breathing room around it survives most crop variations across feed, notification, and shared-post views.

Pairing with your post copy

Keep the mockup as the single hero visual rather than a multi-image carousel for maximum feed impact, then use your post text to explain the one specific outcome or problem solved - the image gets the stop-scroll, the copy gets the read-through.
